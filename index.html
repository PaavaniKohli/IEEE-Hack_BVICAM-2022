Emergency Situations and their Resolve

The Women safety app comprises of three different buttons based on the seriousness of a situation. So, you can tap the one you want depending on your situation.

Minor Emergency

It passes on the user information to the local admin and local team so that the local admin is alerted and is prepared to send help in case of high emergency. It also fakes an incoming call which might scare the predator.

About More

Moderate Emergency

It passes on the user information and user’s current location to the local admin so that the local admin is alerted. The local admin contacts the local female helpline and the local team is deployed to the location of the victim and the user’s current location to all the four emergency contacts.

About More

Major Emergency

Passes the user information directly to the highest authority “the super admin” along with the user’s current location, camera access to the police control room. Police personnel in the area will be alerted, rushing to the victim. The service will be available 24×7.
